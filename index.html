Privacy Policy

Hongshu Trend-Spotting Assistant Browser Extension

Last Updated: January 2025

Thank you for using the Hongshu Trend-Spotting Assistant (hereinafter referred to as "the Extension"). We highly value your privacy and the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy is intended to clearly explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your information. This policy applies to all user information provided through the services of the Extension.

1. Scope of Information Collection

When you use the Extension, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Hongshu Account Information: Only when you actively log in or authorize synchronization, we collect account information for authentication purposes (such as username, profile picture, and other publicly available information).
  • Monitoring and Operational Data: This includes product/note links you add via the Extension, monitoring records, exported results, sidebar operation logs, etc.
  • Extension Usage Data: This includes the frequency of feature usage, preference settings, error logs, etc., to optimize the user experience.
  • Device and Connection Information: This includes basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, operating system version, and extension version number to ensure service stability and risk control identification.

2. Purpose of Information Use

We use the collected information only to the extent necessary to provide the core functionalities of the Extension and continuously improve your experience, including:

  • Executing product and note monitoring tasks and providing you with trend-spotting results.
  • Conducting anonymized statistical analysis to improve monitoring algorithms and risk control mechanisms.
  • Recording your usage behavior through the points system and offering corresponding benefits and services.
  • Identifying and alerting you to risk control statuses such as access restrictions and CAPTCHAs to protect your account security.

3. Information Storage and Protection

  • Your monitoring data and operation records are stored locally on your device by default. Optional synchronization features may use encrypted cloud storage (subject to your explicit authorization).
  • We employ industry-standard technical measures to protect data security and prevent unauthorized access, leakage, or damage.
  • Unless required by law or necessary to provide related services (such as payment verification), we will not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party.

4. User Rights

  • You can view or clear local monitoring records and logs in the Extension's sidebar at any time.
  • If you use cloud synchronization features, you can request data export or deletion through the settings interface.
  • You may choose not to provide certain information, although this may affect the availability of some features.

5. Regarding Hongshu Platform Data

  • The Extension is designed solely to assist you in more efficiently analyzing and tracking publicly available note and product information. All data collection complies with Hongshu platform's relevant agreements and policies.
  • We encourage you to use the Extension responsibly to avoid account restrictions due to excessive frequency or violations of platform rules. We are not responsible for any consequences arising from such situations.

6. Updates to the Privacy Policy

We may revise this policy from time to time. The updated version will take effect upon publication. By continuing to use the Extension, you agree to the revised policy.
